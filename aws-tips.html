aws tips

AWS is one things that you must pick up along the way. Here is a running list of tips that may serve you as well as me.

1. use regex to filter out ec2 loadbalancers

Let's say that you have a 100 numbered load balancers prefixed with the letter 'a' starting at 1, a1, a3, to a100. Now you want to list only a1 and a2. How?

Filter: elb$|elb2$

Right on.
